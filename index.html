Battle Against The Bulge: Conquering Hormonal Weight Gain

Hey there, my name is Elise and I’m about to tell you about my terrifying journey through hormonal weight gain.

The Shocking Reality of Hormonal Weight Gain

In my battle against the bulge, I found myself indulging in unhealthy eating habits. Little did I know, my choices were setting the stage for a tumultuous journey ahead.
The scales slowly crept upwards as I grappled with fluctuating hormones. The consequences were dire - a massive hormonal weight gain.

Unveiling The Enemy Within

Discover the hidden truths behind hormonal weight gain, a silent saboteur lurking beneath the surface of your daily choices!

As my weight continued to climb, I sought the guidance of medical professionals. Visits to gynaecologists, endocrinologists, and nutritionists became my new normal.
Despite their collective efforts, the numbers on the scale refused to budge - I was rapidly gaining even more weight.

Hormone Tests Expose the Culprits Behind Stubborn Weight Gain

Determined to regain control, I underwent numerous hormone tests. The results painted a vivid, albeit scary, picture of hormonal imbalance, with excesses and deficiencies at play.

  • Excess cortisol. Stress had left its mark, with cortisol levels through the roof.
  • Insulin overproduction. My body was overcompensating, producing excessive insulin in response to my poor dietary choices.
  • Thyroid underactivity. The thyroid panel revealed an underactive thyroid, slowing down my body’s natural fat-burning metabolism.

Armed with these results, and finally ready to conquer my hormonal weight gain battle, I embarked on various methods to restore my hormonal balance and shed the stubborn, now reaching unhealthy levels, weight.

Some of the things I’ve tried and were recommended to me by specialists include -

  • Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT). Under the guidance of my endocrinologist, I explored HRT to manage and correct my hormonal imbalance. I started taking oestrogen and progesterone, however, I was experiencing serious side effects such as headaches, unexpected vaginal bleeding, mood changes, mild rashes and hair loss. The bottom line, HRT DID NOT HELP.

  • Balanced diet. I focused on adopting a well-balanced diet that supports hormonal health. From incorporating adequate protein, healthy fats and complex carbohydrates to minimising processed foods and added sugar, I really thought it would work. And yet, ANOTHER DISAPPOINTMENT.

  • Regular exercise. To support my metabolism and, of course, overall well-being, I integrated both cardiovascular and strength training exercises into my routine. This slightly helped in keeping my overall health on the up and up, however, my hormonal weight was NOT GOING DOWN.

  • Stress management. Recognising the impact of stress on my hormonal weight gain, I implemented stress management techniques like meditation, yoga, and deep breathing exercises. Other than the calming effects that followed, I was still GAINING WEIGHT rather than losing it.

  • Adequate sleep. I prioritised getting sufficient and quality sleep since it plays a crucial role in maintaining and supporting hormonal balance. Unfortunately, even with a good night’s sleep, my weight was not lowering.

  • Behavioural changes. I worked hard on addressing my emotional eating patterns and adopted mindful eating practices thinking my weight would normalise and would lose those pesky kilos and yet, THE DESIRED RESULTS DIDN’T COME.

I tried everything I was advised to and, still, the stubborn hormonal weight refused to budge.

I was entering a highly depressive state since nothing worked.
But… after reaching the lowest of low points in my life, I decided a support group would be a perfect way to handle my current mental state.

At first, I was highly sceptic about the whole thing. I didn’t even speak to anyone until the fifth meeting. That’s how I met Louisa.
We started chatting and then after the meeting was over, we decided to take it to the local coffee shop. It was there, after buying a cup of peppermint tea and a juicy strawberry doughnut (don’t judge me, I was on the verge of giving up), she told me about her struggle with rapid weight gain, menopause, and hormonal imbalance.
I couldn’t believe my ears. She was describing my current state and ongoing struggle to a T. I was slack-jawed. Yet, as I was listening to her story, I couldn’t help but notice the lack of hot flashes, fatigue, and overweight.
I couldn’t stop myself from asking - “How are you not sweating out of your eyeballs and not stuffing your face with sweets?
